Benjamin Lurye is an AEA actor, voice-over artist, singer, composer, and music arranger.

Born in Maryland, now based in Brooklyn, Ben has performed at the Tony Award-winning Signature Theatre, Ford's Theatre, The John F. Kennedy Center, Studio Theatre, Rep Stage, UrbanArias, and The Olney Theatre Center.

He has worked for some of the top educational theaters on the east coast, teaches privately, and in his spare time he is a freelance web designer, amateur cook, and adorer of all things fluffy.
