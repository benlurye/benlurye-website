Benjamin Lurye is an AEA actor, singer, composer, and music arranger based in Brooklyn.

Born in Maryland, Ben has performed at the Tony Award-winning Signature Theatre, Ford's Theatre, The John F. Kennedy Center, Studio Theatre, Rep Stage, UrbanArias, and The Olney Theatre Center.

He is also a teaching artist, teaches privately, and in his spare time you can find him cooking up a storm in the kitchen, petting all the fluffy things, or teaching himself to design websites like this one!
