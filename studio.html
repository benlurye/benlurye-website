I am...

...a singer, actor, and teaching artist who has worked with some of the best regional theatres, small orchestras, and arts education organizations in the country.

A classically trained tenor, I hold a BM from the University of Maryland and have spent the last 10+ years working with students of all ages, abilities, and levels of interest.

I started my private studio in 2017, with a mix of amateur and professional students. My high-school aged students have have been accepted to theater programs at NYU, JMU, Elon, Indiana, Ball State, Baldwin Wallace, Penn State, and Pace, and my professional students regularly perform at excellent regional houses, like DC's Shakespeare Theatre Company, 1st Stage, Adventure Theatre MTC, NextStop Theatre, Monumental Theatre Company, and Rep Stage.

My students are...

  • seasoned pros working to revamp their audition books, or to prepare material for a specific performance or audition
  • high schoolers preparing for college (and occasionaly professional) auditions
  • perhaps interested in music theory or composition (understanding theory makes everything easier--trust me!)
  • classically-trained performers wanting to dip their toes into the musical theater or pop world, or vice versa
  • from outside the professional performing world, wanting to sing for fun and relaxation

What we'll do together

After assessing your short- and long-term goals, together we will decide on our areas of focus:

breath, posture, and resonance | repertoire selection | interpretation | music theory | audition preparation |
mental and physical stress and tension reduction techniques | vocal stamina

I am based in Brooklyn, and am currently scheduling online lessons through Zoom. As soon as conditions allow, I will hold in-person sessions in downtown Manhattan and Brooklyn and continue online sessions as necessary, as well.
